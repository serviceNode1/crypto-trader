🔐 How Protection Auto-Execution Works

Setting a protection level = Pre-authorization to sell

  • ✅ When you set a stop loss or take profit level, you're giving permission
  • ✅ The system monitors prices every 5 minutes
  • ✅ When your level is reached, it automatically executes the sell

  • ✅ Trade history shows it as "🛡️ Stop Loss" or "🎯 Take Profit"

💡 Think of it like: Placing a limit order on an exchange. You've already made the decision - execution is just fulfillment.

📤 Full Exit (Default)

✅ DO:

  • Use position sizing - never risk more than 1-2% of capital per trade
  • Follow the trend - "The trend is your friend until it ends"
  • Use technical AND fundamental analysis together

🛡️ Position Sizing Formula

Safe Position Size =
(Account Size × Risk %) ÷ (Entry Price - Stop Loss Price)

Example:
- Account: $10,000
- Risk per trade: 2% = $200
- Entry: $50,000
- Stop Loss: $48,000
- Position Size: $200 ÷ $2,000 = 0.1 BTC
⚠️ Golden Rules:
  • Never risk more than 2% per trade
  • Maximum 20% total portfolio risk at once
  • Larger position = Tighter stop-loss
